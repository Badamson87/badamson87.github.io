About Me

Born and raised in small town Idaho, there is nothing I enjoy more then the outdoors. I am a firm believer that any problem can be solved when viewed from the perspective of a cold night and a warm campfire. I learned the value of a dollar at a young age, working in the family convenience store that also doubled as a service shop. After highschool I attended some college before eventually ending up in the trade industries. After a decade of hard earned growth I found myself ready to tackle life from a new perspective. I'm currently enrolled in Western Governors University pursuing a degree in software development as well as attending classes at Boise Code Works.
